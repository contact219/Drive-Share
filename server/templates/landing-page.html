Why Rush

Everything a rental counter can't do

Rush isn't a rental company — it's a marketplace. Real cars from real people in your neighborhood, with the tech to make every trip effortless and safe.

📍

Cars Where You Are

Browse on the live map and find vehicles parked minutes away — sedans, SUVs, trucks, EVs, and weekend toys.

⚡

Instant Booking

See real-time availability, book in seconds, and coordinate pickup in the in-app chat. No lines, no counters.

🛡️

Covered Every Mile

Insurance protection is built into every trip, and every host and vehicle passes our verification process.

💳

Seamless Payments

Pay securely in the app. Hosts get automatic payouts after every completed trip — no invoicing, no chasing.

⭐

Trust Both Ways

Two-sided reviews keep quality high. Know your host and your car before you ever turn the key.

Your Calendar, Your Rules

How It Works

Two sides. One tap each.

🚗 For Drivers

1
Find your car
Search by location, dates, and vehicle type. Compare prices, photos, and host ratings.
2
Book & verify
Book instantly, upload your license once, and message your host to arrange pickup.
3
Drive & return
Enjoy the trip, return on time, rate your experience. Your next car is already nearby.

🔑 For Hosts

1
List your vehicle
Photos, pricing, and availability — your listing is live after a quick verification.
2
Approve trips
Accept bookings that fit your schedule. Chat with renters right in the app.
3
Get paid
Automatic payouts after each trip — your car earns while you don't need it.
Become a Host

List your vehicle on Rush and turn idle hours into income. You control the price, the calendar, and who drives — we handle booking, payments, verification, and insurance on every trip.
